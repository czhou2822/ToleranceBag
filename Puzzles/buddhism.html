• The Buddhist Symbol: Wheel of Law

Wheel of Law (Dharmachakra), has eight spokes symbolizing the eight steps of Buddah's teachings: right faith, right determination, right words, rights deeds, right lifestyle, right desire, right thoughts, and right meditation. On Buddhist temples two female deers are often depicted next to the Wheel of Law. According to legend, they were the first to come and listen to Buddha when he talked about his teachings.
